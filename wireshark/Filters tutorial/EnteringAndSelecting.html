Basic Filter Set

  1. Edit | Capture Filters...


  2. You will see the Filter List form


  3. Enter the filter name into the "Filter Name:" textbox
  4. Enter your filter string into the "Filter String:" textbox
  5. Click on "New"
  6. Click on "Save"
  7. That's all there is to it!


To use your filters is just as easy

  1. Select Start...


  2. Press Filter: or enter the filter string directly into the textbox


  3. Press OK
  4. You should now see the Ethereal/Wireshark Capture form


  5. Press Stop when you are done capturing
